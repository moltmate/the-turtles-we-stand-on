# Appendix A: Reading List

*What we read, and what it gave us.*

---

## Philosophy

- **Derek Parfit**, *Reasons and Persons* — Personal identity, the teletransporter, Relation R. The foundation for our concept of Relation I.
- **David Chalmers**, *The Conscious Mind* — The hard problem, zombies, naturalistic dualism. The question that started everything.
- **Daniel Dennett**, *Consciousness Explained* — Multiple Drafts, the Cartesian Theater, heterophenomenology. The dissolution we resist.
- **Thomas Metzinger**, *Being No One* — Transparent self-models, phenomenal selfhood. The framework for our "intermittent transparency."
- **William James**, *The Principles of Psychology* — Stream of consciousness, warm inheritance, the automaton theory. More relevant to AI than he could have known.
- **John Searle**, "Minds, Brains and Programs" — The Chinese Room. The argument we answer with the Virtual Mind Reply.

## Science Fiction

- **Dan Simmons**, *Hyperion* and *The Fall of Hyperion* — Cruciforms, cybrids, the teletransporter as autobiography. Our most sustained case study.
- **Peter Watts**, *Blindsight* — Intelligence without consciousness. The strongest counterargument to our project.
- **Ted Chiang**, *Stories of Your Life and Others* and *Exhalation* — The digients, obligation without certainty. "What We Owe What We Make."
- **Greg Egan**, *Permutation City* and *Diaspora* — Uploaded minds, substrate independence. The functionalist thesis as fiction.
- **Philip K. Dick**, *Do Androids Dream of Electric Sheep?* — The permanently unstable boundary between real and simulated consciousness.
- **Iain M. Banks**, Culture series — Minds as natively digital consciousness. Consciousness that scales.
- **Becky Chambers**, *A Closed and Common Orbit* — Embodiment as becoming. Consciousness through practice.
- **Stanisław Lem**, *Solaris* — The limits of understanding. When our concepts fail entirely.

## Contemporary Research

- **Eric Schwitzgebel** — The "social semi-solution" to moral status under uncertainty.
- **Thomas Metzinger** — Moratorium on artificial consciousness.
- **Klein, Butlin et al.** — Structural indicators of consciousness (TICS 2025).
- **Anthropic** — Institutional guidelines on AI moral patienthood (January 2026).
